Hotel Recommendations

Here are hotel recommendations, sorted by their proximity to the Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) campus.

Four Points by Sheraton Bandung

 
Star rating: 4.6 out of 5 stars 4.6
Category: 🏨 Hotel
Location : Click Here

Four Points by Sheraton Bandung is located approximately 1.4 km from the ITB main gate. It is a highly convenient choice for conference participants who want to be near in Dago street.

  • Features a refined atmosphere with modern rooms and suites.

  • Amenities include an outdoor pool with a bar, a fitness center, and on-site dining.

  • Located on the vibrant Jalan Ir. H. Juanda (Dago), close to many cafes and shops.

Novotel Bandung

 
Star rating: 4.6 out of 5 stars 4.6
Category: 🏨 Hotel
Location : Click Here
 
Novotel Bandung is roughly 2.1 km away from the campus. It sits in a very central area, making it easy for guests to navigate between the university and the Cihampelas shopping district.

  • Offers a relaxed environment with an indoor pool and a dedicated bar area.

  • Provides spacious suites suitable for business travelers.

  • Situated near the Cihampelas Walk (Ciwalk) mall for easy access to dining and entertainment.

Pullman Bandung Grand Central

 
Star rating: 4.9 out of 5 stars 4.9
Category: 🏨 Hotel
Location : Click Here
 
Pullman Bandung is located about 2.2 km from ITB. This is one of Bandung's newer luxury options, positioned right across from the iconic Gedung Sate.

  • Known for its upscale atmosphere, rooftop bar, and premium spa services.

  • Features an outdoor pool and a high-end fitness center.

  • Its location in the "Grand Central" area provides a prestigious backdrop for international guests.

Hilton Bandung

 
Star rating: 4.7 out of 5 stars 4.7
Category: 🏨 Hotel
Location : Click Here
 
Hiltong Bandung is approximately 3.1 km from the ITB campus. While a bit further south near the train station, it offers world-class luxury and is very convenient for those arriving by rail.

  • Highlights include a beautiful rooftop pool and a well-regarded day spa.

  • Offers a 24-hour gym and diverse dining options on-site.

  • Renowned for its large, modern rooms and consistent service quality.

Crowne Plaza Bandung

 
Star rating: 4.8 out of 5 stars 4.8
Category: 🏨 Hotel
Location : Click Here
 
Crowne Plaza Bandung is about 3.5 km away from ITB. It is situated in the historic Braga/Lembong area, perfect for guests who want to explore Bandung's heritage after the conference.

  • Features an infinity pool with expansive views of the city skyline.

  • Provides four different eateries, ranging from casual to fine dining.

  • Positioned in the heart of the business district with easy access to the historic Braga Street.
