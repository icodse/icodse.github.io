2026 IEEE ICoDSE

2026 IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) 

ICoDSE (International Conference on Data and Software Engineering) is an international conference focused on the latest developments in data and software engineering. It brings together researchers, practitioners, and professionals to share knowledge on topics such as big data processing, software development, artificial intelligence, and more.

ICoDSE 2026, the 12th edition of the conference, will focus on Generative AI seen from the lens of practitioners and the body of software and data engineering. The conference will explore how generative AI and other AI technology enhances secure systems development through intelligent code generation, intelligent test automation, and synthetic data creation. It will also emphasize the importance of solid data and software engineering foundations & clean data pipelines, scalable architectures, and robust deployment practices in supporting AI applications for secure systems. This symbiotic relationship forms a cycle where generative AI advances of software/data engineering, and vice versa.

Venue
Aula Barat, Institut Teknologi Bandung
