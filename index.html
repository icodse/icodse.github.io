Call for Papers

International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) 2021

To be held online
3-4 November 2021

“Data and Software Engineering for Supporting Sustainable Development Goals”

This joint effort of Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB), Indonesia and Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), The Netherlands will include keynotes from Prof. Benhard Sitohang (ITB), Prof. A Min Tjoa (Vienna University of Technology), and Christoph Treude, Ph.D. (The University of Adelaide).

Important dates:

  • Early Submission Deadline: 1 June 2021
  • Early Notification of Acceptance: 22 June 2021
  • Paper Submission Deadline: 7 September 2021
  • Notification of Acceptance: 28 September 2021
  • Camera Ready Deadline: 12 October 2021
  • Conference Dates: 3-4 November 2021

Data/knowledge engineering and software engineering are two developing areas in information and communication technology, which are interrelated and have significant roles in changing the world in many aspects, such as business, education, government, and society. Although these disciplines are pretty mature, research and development in these disciplines are still evolving to provide better performance of offered services or products in different criteria, e.g., efficiency or effectiveness.

2021 International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE), the seventh of a conference series started in 2014, will bring together scientists, educators, engineers, and practitioners from multiple disciplines to focus on engineering complex software system and data-intensive software systems. The conference will feature world-class keynotes and research & industry presentations to discuss high-quality research results and industry experiences in this field.
