Conference Registration

Authors of accepted papers must guarantee that their paper will be presented at the conference. Every paper accepted for 2025 IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) must have an associated registration at the full member or non-member author rate. At least one author of each accepted paper must be registered for the conference by October 21th, 2025 in order for that paper to appear in the proceedings and to be scheduled for presentation.

Page limit

The page limit for regular (full) papers is six (6) pages. Please use the pages in full papers for addressing the comments of the reviewers, make your figures large enough to be legible, and to comply with all formatting requirements. Please note that it is not possible to purchase extra pages.

Camera-Ready Paper Upload

Before uploading your paper, please:

  1. Please check that the title and abstract in EasyChair match your camera-ready paper exactly. If it is different, please send an email to 2025 IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) chair (icodse@informatika.org).
  2. Please use Title Case for the paper title, i.e. capitalizing the first letters of all main words.
  3. Compare all author names in EasyChair with the author list in your paper. They must be identical and in the same order.
  4. Enter all middle names/initials you prefer to use in the paper in the author profile at EasyChair as well.
  5. Ensure that special characters in the paper match the EasyChair entry.
  6. Have the PDF checked or created by IEEE PDF eXpressTM; upload the certified file returned from IEEE PDF eXpressTM into EasyChair. Please refer to the information provided in section on IEEE PDF eXpressTM.
  7. Put together the auxiliary files (Microsoft Word documents, Latex documents, figures, etc.) that are necessary to build the camera-ready pdf file into one zip file. Send the zip file to icodse@informatika.org. The zip file must not be corrupted and should be able to be extracted/opened properly.

This is very important because we will use the information provided in EasyChair to generate the final conference program and the table of contents for IEEE XploreÂ®ï¸. Please note that the abstract and title should be readable on its own and not contain any references or unexplained acronyms.

In case you need to modify the title or the abstract, you can do this through EasyChair. Changes to the list of authors, allowed only under exceptional circumstances, and have to be approved by TPC chairs. All the camera-ready papers have to be submitted via EasyChair by October 21th, 2025.

Presentation Video for Online Presenters

The conference will be held offline & online. For online presentation, presenters are expected to present live during the conference dates. However, in preparation for possible technical difficulties, presenters are required to upload pre-recorded presentation video. The duration for the video is max. 15 minutes. Please note that at least one author of each paper must still join the virtual conference room for live Q&A session.

This means that the papers will be available within IEEE XploreÂ®ï¸ without any charges or subscriptions after 12 months.

Therefore, authors are required to transfer the copyright of their accepted papers to IEEE. This has to be done by submitting the IEEE copyright form, with further instructions that will be emailed separately. Note that all papers will stay under IEEE Copyright.
