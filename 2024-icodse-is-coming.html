Social Events

Notes for the social events:
- They are two options to watch the whale sharks: watching from the boat, or swimming with them in the sea (yes, they are big, but friendly).
- Please do not touch the whale sharks, because they have sensitive skins.
- Snorkeling gears, fins, and life vests are available if you need them.
- Changing rooms and bathrooms are also available.
