DSE Day Pitching Competition

We invite students and the general public to participate for free in our pre-event Pitching Competition! Win exciting prizes and a chance to meet potential investors for your digital business idea.

Competitors may register as individuals or groups of 5 max. Submit your proposal, written in two pages of A4 paper, by 28 October 2021 through the following form: https://forms.gle/3WFfPh2BeL7sjRfr5.

Competitors will present their proposal (@10 mins.) before the board of judges in a virtual meeting on 30 October 2021.

This competition is a part of DSE Day, a pre-event to the ICoDSE 2021.
