CALL FOR PAPERS
2025 IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE)
📅 October 28–29, 2025 | 📍 Batam, Indonesia (Hybrid)
🌐 https://icodse.org | 📩 icodse@informatika.org

Dear Colleagues,

We are delighted to invite you to the 2025 IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) — a prestigious, IEEE-indexed event that brings together global researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to discuss groundbreaking advances in software systems, data, and intelligent technologies.

Organized by:

  • School of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, Institut Teknologi Bandung (STEI ITB)
  • Politeknik Negeri Batam
  • Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS (UTP)

Also co-organized by:

  • Universitas Maritim Raja Ali Haji

Conference Theme:
“Generative AI Meets Data and Software Engineering: Challenges and Innovations”

This year’s edition focuses on the intersection between Generative AI and Software/Data Engineering, exploring how GenAI transforms development workflows and data pipelines, and how advancements in these fields drive the future of AI.

🎤 Keynote Speakers:

  • Assoc. Prof. Christoph Treude – SMU, Singapore
  • Univ. Prof. Marta Sabou, Ph.D. – WU, Austria
  • Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ir. G.A. Putri Saptawati, M.Comm. – ITB, Indonesia
  • Shrinath Thube – IBM Software Developer and IEEE Senior Member

Special Session by Dr. Ganesh Kumar: Generative AI for Education — Transforming Learning, Teaching, and Assessment
This special session will explore how Generative AI technologies are revolutionizing education from personalized learning and curriculum design to adaptive assessment, intelligent tutoring, and ethical considerations in AI-powered classrooms. Topics include AI assisted content creation, LLM based student support, learning analytics, educational chatbots, and ensuring data privacy and fairness in AI tools for education.
