Call For Papers 2026

📢 CALL FOR PAPERS | ICoDSE 2026

The 12th IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering

"Next Generation of Intelligent Secure Systems"

📍 Location: Bandung, Indonesia (Hybrid Venue)

🗓️ Dates: 21–22 October 2026

🌐 Website: https://icodse.org

📧 Contact: icodse@informatika.org

Overview

The 2026 IEEE ICoDSE is a premier international forum for researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to share the latest innovations in software systems and data engineering. As we navigate the era of autonomous systems and pervasive AI, this year’s theme of Next Generation of Intelligent Secure Systems focuses on the intersection of robust engineering, data-driven intelligence, and cybersecurity.

We invite high-quality, original submissions that address contemporary challenges in building reliable, scalable, and secure digital ecosystems.

Important Dates

All deadlines are 23:59 AoE (Anywhere on Earth)

  • Paper Submission Deadline: 7 August 2026  
    • Notification of Acceptance: 7 September 2026  
    • Registration: 8-21 September 2026
    • Camera-Ready: 21 September 2026  
  • Conference Dates: 21–22 October 2026

Technical Tracks & Topics

We welcome submissions in three primary tracks, with a special emphasis on (but not limited to) AI-driven security and data privacy.

Track 1: Intelligent Software Systems

  • AI-Augmented Engineering: AI for SE, SE for AI-Enabled Systems, Generative AI in DevSecOps
  • Process & Quality: Agile, Lean, and Empirical Software Engineering, Software Testing & Verification
  • Modern Architectures: Cloud-Native Systems, Microservices, and Edge Computing
  • Human & Organizational Factors: SE Education, Requirements Engineering, UX/UI Design

Track 2: Data-Driven Intelligence

  • Data Intelligence: Data Science, Knowledge Discovery (KDD), Predictive Analytics, and Machine Learning Systems
  • Large-Scale Data Systems: Big Data Technologies, Distributed Databases, Cloud Data Warehousing, and Real-Time Streaming
  • Knowledge Representation: Semantic Web, Ontologies, Knowledge Graphs, and Linked Data
  • Analytics & Visualization: Visual Analytics, Spatial/Temporal Data Management, and Explainable AI

Track 3: Trustworthy & Secure Systems

  • Secure Software Engineering: Secure Architectures, Vulnerability Analysis, Static/Dynamic Analysis, and Penetration Testing
  • Data Security & Privacy: Privacy-Preserving Data Mining, Synthetic Data Generation, Data Governance, and Differential Privacy
  • Decentralized & Resilient Systems: Blockchain-based Systems, Distributed Trust, and Fault-Tolerant Architectures
  • Emerging Threats: AI-Driven Threat Detection, Adversarial ML, and Cybersecurity for Autonomous Systems

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: Submissions must be original, unpublished, and follow the standard IEEE Conference Template (A4, 2-column).
  • Page Limit: Full papers should be between 4 to 6 pages, including figures and references.
  • Peer Review: All submissions will undergo a rigorous double-blind peer-review process.
  • Publication: Accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore, subject to meeting IEEE’s scope and quality requirements.
