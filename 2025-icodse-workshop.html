2025 ICoDSE Workshop

As part of the 2025 IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE), we proudly present a hands-on workshop titled
“Practical LLMs: Utilizing Language Models for Industry Challenges.”
This two-day workshop will take place on September 20–21, 2025 at Technopark, Politeknik Negeri Batam, featuring expert mentors:

  • Hari Bagus Firdaus (Radya Labs)
  • Agung Dewandaru (STEI ITB)
  • Vaibhav Tupe (Equinix)

Participants will gain insights into the fundamentals and advanced prompting of LLMs, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), as well as hands-on sessions with local LLMs and multimodal integrations using n8n.

This workshop offers valuable benefits including guidance from experienced AI practitioners, an official certificate from STEI ITB, networking and collaboration opportunities, live demos, hands-on project templates, and real-world case studies on corporate document RAG. Registration is now open with a special early-bird price for the first 10 participants. Secure your spot today through the following registration link: https://forms.gle/ahadAeKKZpGzto4W8.
