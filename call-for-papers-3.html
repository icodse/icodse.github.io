IEEE International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) is an annual event organized by the Knowledge and Software Engineering Research Group (KSE), School of Electrical Engineering and Informatics (STEI), ITB, since 2014. Several editions of ICoDSE have been conducted in collaboration with other universities in Indonesia. The year 2024 marks the 10th edition of ICoDSE, in collaboration with State University of Gorontalo, Indonesia.

Data/knowledge engineering and software engineering are two developing areas in information and communication technology, which are interrelated and have significant roles in changing the world in many aspects, such as business, education, government, and society. Although these disciplines are pretty mature, research and development in these disciplines are still evolving to provide better performance of offered services or products in different criteria, e.g., efficiency or effectiveness.

2024 IEEE ICoDSE will bring together scientists, educators, engineers, and practitioners from multiple disciplines to focus on engineering complex software system and data-intensive software systems. The conference will feature world-class keynotes and research & industry presentations to discuss high-quality research results and industry experiences in this field.
