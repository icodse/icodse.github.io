Call for Papers

8th International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE) 2022

Denpasar, Bali, Indonesia
November 2-3, 2022

“Data Engineering and Software Engineering in the Era of Metaverse”

Keynote speakers:

  • Prof. Benhard Sitohang (Bandung Institute of Technology, Indonesia)
  • Prof. A Min Tjoa (Vienna University of Technology, Austria)
  • Prof. Hendro Wicaksono (Jacobs University, Germany).
  • Dr. Giovanni Rusello (University of Auckland, New Zealand).

Important dates:

  • Early Submission Deadline: 15 June 2022
  • Early Notification of Acceptance: 24 June 2022
  • Paper Submission Deadline: 12 September 2022
  • Notification of Acceptance: 23 September 2022
  • Early bird: 23 September - 28 September 2022
  • Camera Ready Deadline: 11 October 2022
  • Conference Dates: 2-3 November 2022

Data/knowledge engineering and software engineering are two developing areas in information and communication technology, which are interrelated and have significant roles in changing the world in many aspects, such as business, education, government, and society. Although these disciplines are pretty mature, research and development in these disciplines are still evolving to provide better performance of offered services or products in different criteria, e.g., efficiency or effectiveness.

8th International Conference on Data and Software Engineering (ICoDSE 2022) will bring together scientists, educators, engineers, and practitioners from multiple disciplines to focus on engineering complex software system and data-intensive software systems. The conference will feature world-class keynotes and research & industry presentations to discuss high-quality research results and industry experiences in this field.
