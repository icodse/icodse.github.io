Venue

Technopark Politeknik Negeri Batam (Polibatam)

Technopark Polibatam is an innovation and technology development hub located in the strategic area of Batam, Riau Islands. Established to foster collaboration between industry, academia, and government, Technopark Polibatam offers modern facilities such as conference halls, technology laboratories, coworking spaces, and product innovation exhibition areas. Designed to inspire creativity and collaboration, it serves as an ideal ecosystem for research activities, product development, and knowledge exchange across various fields, including data science and software engineering. Its close proximity to Batam’s industrial zone makes Technopark a vital bridge between academic innovation and real-world business needs.

As the venue for ICoDSE 2025, Technopark Polibatam provides a professional and inspiring conference experience. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, high-speed internet connectivity, and a comfortable environment, it is well-suited for presentations, scientific discussions, and networking among international researchers. Attendees will also benefit from easy access to nearby hotels, culinary spots, and local attractions. Another key advantage is its location—just a short distance from Batam’s international ferry terminals and Hang Nadim Airport—making it convenient for both domestic and international participants, including those planning short trips to Singapore or Malaysia.
